# Brand Palette Guide (first section)

## Non-negotiables
- Use ONE primary dark background and ONE primary light text.
- Use ONE accent color (sparingly) for links, buttons, and highlights.
- Keep contrast high (readability > aesthetics).
- Avoid gradients and heavy shadows; keep it crisp and “research/tech”.

## Palette (practical defaults)
- Background: #0B0F19
- Surface (cards/sections): #111827
- Text: #E5E7EB
- Muted text: #9CA3AF
- Accent (links/buttons): #7C3AED
- Success/positive (rare): #10B981
- Warning (rare): #F59E0B

## Usage rules
- Body text always in Text or Muted text.
- Links always Accent; underline on hover.
- Buttons: Accent background, Text color.
- Sections/cards use Surface; never use pure black (#000).
